1. What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored in your browser when you visit almost any website. Its utility is that the web is able to remember your visit when you return to browse that page.

2. What cookies does this website use?

This website uses only technical and personalization cookies, which are essential for the operation of the site. We do not use third-party, advertising, or behavioral analysis cookies.

  • cookie-consent: Local Storage to remember if you have accepted or declined the cookie notice. Duration: Permanent (until you clear the cache).

3. Consent

Since we do not use tracking or advertising cookies, regulations allow the use of technical cookies without explicit consent, although we inform you of their use for transparency.
